About CSHS

Focused on empowering students with computer science skills to better serve our community today and improve the future

CSHS is an organization that promotes learning about Computer Science. We believe that learning to code can help you improve your own life and lead to successful careers. Also, coding is something we think you should enjoy learning. If these traits sound like something that descrobe you, join us here at CSHS or InnovateCS.

Focused on empowering students with computer science skills

One of CSHS's main goals is to help people learn to code. We want to give all students resources to explore computer science and the careers that come with it.

to better serve our community today

Another goal of CSHS is to serve our community using CS. We want to contribute to our, school, local, and online communities. To learn more about ways we do this, go to our projects page.

and improve the future

Finally, here at CSHS, we want to help build a better tommorow. We all know that CS is becoming more and more involved in our lives, so we want to help people learn to use it for the better. The more people that know CS, the more society can use it, and the more helpful it can be.
